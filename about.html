About Us

Meet Our Company and Seize the Opportunity

At Aid Finance, we offer innovative financial solutions that empower individuals and businesses to achieve success. As a registered credit provider (NCRCP17839), we are committed to delivering seamless experiences across South Africa, through our online platform, branches, and WhatsApp line. Our vision is clear: to establish the first fully people-centric bank in South Africa and expand across Southern Africa within 5 to 10 years, with plans to reach the entire continent and make a global impact within 25 years.

Aid Finance believes that banks do not inherently possess wealth but act as managers of people's and entities' resources. This belief fuels our mission to create products and services that prioritize the needs of individuals while fostering impactful socio-economic programs that serve society. Our CEO, Nkosinathi Mdlovu, is leading this transformative journey, dedicated to reshaping the banking landscape by delivering community-focused solutions and innovative financial services that bring value and growth to all stakeholders.
